Welcome to the eloquence contest that we have organized in collaboration with the Sorbonne Paris Nord University located at the Villetaneuse campus.

This site will allow you to have all the necessary information about the eloquence contest of June 17, 2022. Moreover, if you wish you can relive the contest of April 14, 2022 by clicking on the on the heading "Contest". The last section "Team" is a presentation of the different members of the members of the organization as well as their roles within the project.

Presentation of the contest

The eloquence contest will take place on June 17 at the IUT of Villetaneuse in the right amphitheater at 11 am. This year, in reference to the last anthology competition, the subject of this year will be "The metaverse, life is elsewhere ?" .

Introduction of the candidates

This year's candidates are the different students pre-selected by the teachers through the teachers through the tests and classes of the last month before the competition. Each class will be represented by 2 members, they will have the honor to face the winners of the last competition (Tinihane and Kais).

Contest (prizes to be won)

This competition will also have its share of surprises to reward the public through various questions, throughout the competition with very large prizes as rewards

Course of the competition

The competition will be held in the traditional way, the whole promotion will be invited to invited to support the participants. The jury will be composed of communication professors. communication, the competition will be broadcast on the social networks of the university social networks (live Instagram) thanks to the different cameramen and their teams present on on site.

Public Survey

The public will also have a role to play in this competition. A poll will be carried out in order to vote for the participants having realized the best performance. Their vote will count for 1/3 of the final vote. The remaining 2/3 will be attributed to the jury. The vote will be done on Menti.com.

Announcement of the winners of the contest

Following the different passages, the jury will take its time to their votes. This will allow to calculate the different votes of the public and then add the two results to obtain the results. The presenter will announce the votes and give the awards to the top 3.

To conclude the competition in a good mood, a snack will be organized with the staff, teachers and participants. This will be followed by interviews and sharing between the participants and the public.
